DNA is the molcule

DNA has a backbone of sugar and phosphates on either side of the molecule. In the center, there are pairs of four nitrogenous bases, adenineadenine, thymine, cytosine, and guanine. These are represendaed by the letteres A, T, C, and G repectively.
